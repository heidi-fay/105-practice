Hi, my name is Heidi and I have some advice for you!

EVERYTHING WILL BE GARBAGE FOR A WHILE AND THAT'S OKAY.

You are learning SEVERAL new languages BY YOURSELF which were basically INVENTED in the last hundred years and constantly evolve. Nobody hands you a newsletter when something changes in tech, you just have to keep up.

The only thing that matters is movement. Keep going. One lesson, half a lesson, anything. Read an article, google a concept you're unclear on. If you start hating one part of it, take a break and learn something else- brush up on photoshop. The knowledge to be gained is endless.

And that's where things get hard.

There is so much to learn. It can get overwhelming really fast. I'm hyperventilating just thinking about it. So don't.

This isn't a race. Learn it, retain it, enjoy it. And forget some of it. Cause that's what people do. That's what all these freaking bookmarks are for dude.
